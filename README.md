# PocketFriendly
(PROJECT) A smart budget tracker that provides important details such as spending insights. 
 ## Welcome to PocketFriendly!
 Do you have trouble saving or keeping track of your spendings and earnings? Do not worry, **PocketFriendly** is here to help you get back on track!

With this budget tracker, the you should be able to:
1. Gain control over their finances.
2. Practice better saving habits.
3. Increase their financial awareness and planning.

Key Features of this application include:
1. Dashboard and UI that is easy to use and navigate through, providing useful information such as transaction history and spending summary.
2. Smart insights to notify the user about their spending habits and alert them incase of overspending.
3. Additional budget limit setter to track how your savings/overspending compares to how much you initially planned to use and save.
4. Pie Chart to visalize all of your expenses and how they compare to eachother.
5. Search, edit and delete transactions to remove outdated transactions, fix mistakes and easily track down specific transactions.

## How To Use
The application is easy to use. Here is a rundown of how to operate it:
- ### Adding Transactions 
 Simply input your transaction amount, its category (such as Food, Business, etc.) and select whether it was an expense or income. After clicking the "Add Transaction" button, the transaction details and date will be saved. You will be notified immediately if an error occurs. You will also be given an alert incase any expense you add exceeds your set budget.
- ### Insights 
In the insights menu, use the provided input section to set your desired budget. After that, you can view the insight details in this section. In case you exceed your budget, you can also view it here. Additionally, it provides insights on your spending habits to notify you whether you are on the path of overspending, or if you are saving. 
- ### Transaction History
This section shows every transaction added, with details such as the category, amount, type, and the date added. Next to each transaction is a pencil button used to edit the type, category and amount, and a delete button to get rid of the transaction. There is also a search bar used to easily locate transactions by searching the amount,type,category or date/time.

- ### Spending Breakdown
This displays every single expense in pie chart form, allowing you to easily visualize your expenses and how each expense compares to each other. With this, you can easily plan your expenses to avoid overspending and to find out what you are not spending enough on.
